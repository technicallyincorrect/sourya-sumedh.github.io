01

PROGRAM SCALING · CONTRACT GOVERNANCE · CROSS-FUNCTIONAL LEADERSHIP

Scaled transformation of Enhanced Software Evergreen Services

Transformed a fragmented subscription-delivery program into a standardized, contract-driven operating model for continuous software updates.

Role
Program Manager, North American Professional Services
Business model
3-5 year continuous software subscription lifecycle
Impact
Scaled delivery capacity by 50% and safeguarded $3M in contract value

Challenge

No centralized contract tracking, ad-hoc implementation practices, unclear handoffs, and high risk of unbilled overages.

Action

Built a governed delivery framework with intake gates, parallel workstreams, consumption tracking, SOPs, and escalation paths.

Result

Created a scalable subscription program with clearer ownership, faster deployments, and stronger margin protection.

Read the detailed case study

Situation

The organization moved from standalone project delivery to a continuous software update model covering operating systems, applications, branding, and marketing services. Delivery needed to respect strict contractual guardrails, including limited major upgrade builds, annual refreshes, and predefined update entitlements.

Operating Model

I standardized the lifecycle into five phases: intake and trigger, kick-off and scope validation, execution, validation and deployment, and closure and tracking. A build-versus-refresh gate clarified when a request was a simple update versus a resource-intensive core rebuild.

Execution Architecture

To remove linear bottlenecks, I introduced parallel execution across CXM updates, branding updates, shared properties, and copy-file packages. Each stream followed the same repeatable flow, giving engineering teams consistency without slowing them down.

Governance

I launched a central QuickBase tracker for eligibility, annual usage, and consumed upgrade credits. Scope thresholds halted out-of-contract work and redirected accounts to Sales when add-on or upsell handling was required.

Enablement

Standard kick-off kits, requirement triage workflows, KPI dashboards, SOP flowcharts, and walkthrough documentation helped Professional Services, Managed Services, technical SMEs, Sales, and customers work from the same source of truth.

Business Impact

The program scaled without added headcount, eliminated unbilled overages on core platform builds, improved update predictability, reduced handover friction, and gave customers a clearer subscription experience.
